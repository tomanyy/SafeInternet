Our Mission

SafeWatch is a user protection initiative focused on maintaining safety across online social platforms like Roblox, VRChat, and Discord.

Our mission is to detect and prevent harmful behavior by identifying patterns, linking users across platforms, and empowering moderators and communities with actionable data.

Why We Exist

  • To reduce the spread of harmful or abusive behavior
  • To support victims and community leaders
  • To promote transparency and ethical online conduct

Our Approach

  1. Gather and analyze behavioral reports
  2. Link users across accounts and platforms
  3. Provide tools to community moderators
  4. Collaborate with platform safety teams when appropriate
