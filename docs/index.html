What is SafeWatch?

SafeWatch is a community-driven platform dedicated to improving digital safety on platforms like Roblox, VRChat, Discord, and more. Our goal is to verify and expose abusive behavior, protect users, and equip moderators with powerful, transparent tools.

Cross-Platform Tracking

Monitor user activity across multiple platforms and stay ahead of harmful behavior trends.

Verified Reports

Community reports are carefully vetted by trained moderators to ensure accuracy and fairness.

Privacy & Transparency

We prioritize ethical moderation by putting transparency and user privacy at the forefront.
