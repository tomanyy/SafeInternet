What is SafeWatch?

SafeWatch is a community-driven platform focused on digital safety across social gaming networks and chat platforms. We aim to track, verify, and expose abusive behavior and empower moderators with unified tools.

Cross-Platform Tracking

Track user behavior across Roblox, VRChat, Discord, and more. Stay informed across ecosystems.

Community Reports

Users can submit misconduct anonymously. Reports are verified by trained moderators.

Transparency First

We value privacy, transparency, and accountability in everything we publish.
